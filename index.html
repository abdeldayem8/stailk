Stailk is a smart shopping platform designed to solve the challenges of online clothing purchases. By creating a personalized 3D avatar based on your body measurements, you can virtually try on outfits and see how they fit before buying. Our app allows seamless shopping from multiple stores in one order and helps you mix and match items to ensure a perfect look. With a virtual closet feature, you can coordinate new outfits with clothes you already own. Stailk is your ultimate solution for confident and convenient online shopping!
